Coding became a passion of mine within days. I am currently pursing a career in computer programming through the CodeCrew coding boot camp. I spend my days solving puzzles and my nights experimenting with code I don't yet understand. One week into the boot camp I knew that I had found my true passion. I have a background in design and concern myself with aesthetic and content equally. I look to combine the sometimes frustrating rationality of code with the always frustrating subjectivity of design to reach beautiful solutions to complex problems. I've spent my whole life in Memphis and this vibrant city and surrounding towns offer inspiration for everyone at every turn. I aspire to tap into the pulsing vein of computer programming that runs throughout the city and add to its energy tenfold.
