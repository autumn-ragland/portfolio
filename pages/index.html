I started my journey to a career in technology in January of 2019 and since then I have been learning, growing and teaching in my hometown of Memphis, TN. I learned the fundamentals of development as a student in the second cohort of CodeCrew Code School from January to July of 2019. After graduation I interned at International Paper working on an Angular application used in the paper mills. I am currently a co-instructor for Code School's boot camp and the primary instructor for the part time program. I have served as a member of the Code Connector leadership board since February 2020. I've also had the privilege to participate in Give Camp Memphis and the Memphis Civic Data Hack-a-thon.
